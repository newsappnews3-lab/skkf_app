Privacy Policy for SKKF App

Last updated: February 2026

This privacy policy describes how the SKKF app (“we”, “the app”) handles information when you use the application.

1. Information we collect and use

  • Account and profile: When you sign in or create a profile, we store your name, phone number, email (if provided), and date of birth (if provided) in our secure database to provide the app’s services.
  • Contacts (Android): The app can access your device contacts only when you choose “Pick from contacts” (e.g. when an admin adds a member). We use this solely to let you select a contact to add; we do not upload or store your full contact list on our servers. You can deny this permission and still use the app by entering details manually.
  • Notifications: If you enable notifications, we store a device token so we can send you relevant alerts (e.g. loan issued, repayment recorded).

2. How we store and protect data

Your data is stored using Firebase (Google) services. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Your choices

You can revoke contact access in your device settings at any time. You can opt out of notifications in the app or device settings.

4. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact the app administrator or your organisation.
